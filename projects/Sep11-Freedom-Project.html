Sep 11 Freedom Project

The “Freedom Project” for SEP11 is all about creating something that will help others get better at a skill or help in real-life situations. The purpose of this blog is to see all the progress we learn and how we got to our final product.

For my project, I am making a shooting game. This blog will walk through my process of independently studying on phasers in order to make a space shooting game that has a crosshair and you allow to shoot and gain points.

Some challenges I have working with Phaser was the animation I have to find a sprite sheet that work and lower the size putting into where you want to play animation was hard.
